## Default Discord Posts

### Pass

```text
**:tada: Congratulations! Great job! You have successfully completed your test calls. Watch your inbox for your step 4 final instructions - your Welcome Team TLMS information. Please log out of CallCorp and Simple Script with the Log Out links (never X out of these windows). Welcome to ACDD! Have a fabulous remainder of your day! • If you haven't already done so be sure to complete any additional courses assigned in your TLMS. Email certification@acdsupport.com for any questions on courses. • If you're signed up on any additional mock or testing shifts, please take a moment to go to Current Schedule under My Schedule in Gateway and remove them.
**Welcome to ACDD! Have a fabulous remainder of your day!** 

```

### Fail Session

```text
It was a pleasure working with you today. Unfortunately, based on our session, I can’t pass you. I think you will benefit from further practice in the WXYZ Certification script. Please reschedule for another Mock session in Gateway as soon as possible (within 24 hours).

If you don’t see a shift, please check back periodically as the shifts will be added as soon as possible to your Shifts Calendar. You can also check out the test chat room and if we have time, we will post a message – you can come in and we can help you then.

```

### Fail Final Attempt

```text
Thank you for your time and interest in becoming a phone agent for ACD Direct and it was a pleasure working with you today. Unfortunately, based on our session, I'm unable to pass you. At this time you have exceeded the amount of Mock Call session attempts allowed. We wish you luck in your future endeavors and have a nice day.
.
```

### Out of Time

```text
Our time has come to an end for this shift. We will need to schedule a time to meet again to complete your Supervisor Transfers. You will not need to schedule anything on your end. 
Please give me one moment to check my availability.
```

### Ncns

```text
Candidate was a No Call / No Show for mock testing.
```

### Sup Intro

```text
I'll help you complete the Supervisor Transfer test call. I'll provide instructions step by step. OK?
```

### Sup Instructions

```text
When you need to transfer:
1) Ask in chat first
2) Give CCM time to check
3) When CCM says ok - let caller know you are transferring
```

### Sup Transfer

```text
1. Click Transfer in CC
2. Select Queue > ACD Direct Supervisor
3. Tell caller to hold, click Blind Transfer
```

### Sup Dte

```text
Change DTE to Ready (green).
```

### Sup Status

```text
I show Ready. Calling now.
```

### Sup Transfer Now

```text
You may transfer the call now.
```

### Sup Stars Post

```text
WXYZ: Supervisor Test Call Being Queued
```

### Sup Disposition

```text
Click cancel (red phone), disposition as Test/Training.
Then disposition in Call Corp.
```

### Sup Retry

```text
Transfer was not successful. Review steps and let me know when ready.
```
